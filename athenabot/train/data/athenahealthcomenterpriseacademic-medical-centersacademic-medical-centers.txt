Helping Academic Medical Centers grab the mantle of change.
Academic Medical Centers nationwide are sitting in the eye of a turbulent storm. Your mission, along with your margins, are being challenged every day by declining reimbursement and utilization, shrinking research funding, and expanding Medicaid programs..
athenahealth's cloud-based services help you address your most pressing challenges: physician and patient loyalty, population health management, staying competitive locally, and achieving clinical integration with employed and affiliated physicians..
The athenahealth Advantage.
Fast time-to-value with no capital expense, low start-up costs, and zero maintenance fees.
Complete network-level visibility and control, enabling continuous performance improvement and proactive intervention.
A nimble, innovative platform to keep you prepared for industry change today, and in the future.
Seamless care coordination across settings and disparate systems, with a fully integrated patient access and clinical integration service.
