Real-time communication for care teams.
This is care coordination you can carry anywhere. athenaText enables HIPAA-compliant information exchange between providers, across the hall or across town. Coordinating exceptional care, without exceptions..
Easy, immediate care collaboration on the go.
When you need consults that don’t disrupt care and can’t wait until the end of the day, athenaText — our secure text messaging service — is the solution..
Share essential information and get critical answers in the moment of care.
Build efficient care teams with easy, real-time communication between providers.
Text whenever, wherever, from your mobile phone, Apple Watch, and online within the athenahealth network.
Watch demo.
Share patient information without risking patient privacy “Tall Giraffe needs drink of water.” How cryptic have your text messages been to protect personal health information (PHI)? Now, with our secure text messaging service, clinicians and staff can exchange clear and complete messages while staying HIPAA compliant. By removing guesswork from important conversations, we're helping physicians concentrate on their patients in the moments of care..
Send accurate and complete patient information, including lab results and radiology and other images, on a HIPAA-compliant platform.
Thorough security technology: Message encryption, unique user identification, user authentication, app auto-lock, data integrity controls and more enable you to comply with HIPAA and HITECH.
Answer your athenaText Security FAQs here ..
Connect to your closest colleagues and over 1,000,000 providers.
With over 1 million health care providers and staff in the athenaText network, it's easy to make a connection, request a second opinion, and improve outcomes for your patients..
Organize patient-centered group chats across care teams to expedite information delivery and prevent duplicate work.
Get instant access to our entire Provider Directory of medical professionals, powered by Epocrates.
Free Download: Get athenaText for your iOS or Android mobile device..
Find and share drug information with a click.
Epocrates drug information is embedded directly into athenaText to save you time and surface the latest drug information whenever you need it. The two services work in tandem to help providers focus on the moments of care..
No need to switch screens or devices to ensure your messages have accurate dosing and contraindications — just click drug names as you text.
Improve patient care and safety with Epocrates information at your fingertips.
Communicate prescribing information to your clinical staff easily and instantly.
