Dr. Her vs. Dr. Him: Gender Differences in Physician Engagement.
Now Available On Demand This Valentine’s Day, join us as we explore gender disparities in healthcare, from engagement to patient outcomes. We’ll share findings from provider data on the athenahealth® network, and offer strategies you can implement to drive greater physician satisfaction..
About the presenter.
Jessica Sweeney-Platt is Executive Director of Marketing at athenahealth. From 2015 to 2017, she served as Executive Director on the athenaResearch team, analyzing data from across athenahealth’s platforms to uncover best practices in client performance, and sharing these findings with clients and the industry at large. Previously, she was a Managing Director with The Advisory Board Company, a Washington, D.C.-based healthcare research and technology provider..
