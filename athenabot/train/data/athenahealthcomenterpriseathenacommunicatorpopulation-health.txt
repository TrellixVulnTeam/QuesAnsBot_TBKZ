The athenahealth advantage.
It’s about results..
We track performance and look for opportunities to drive better financial and clinical outcomes. Our clients consistently outperform industry averages in key population health metrics..
Works with your EHR. And every other EHR..
Access clinical data in a single, longitudinal patient record, from payer claims to pharmacy data, lab data, and more. We’ll work with any EHR vendor to surface the right data and make it actionable..
Empower your patients with less work..
No more unreturned phone calls. With athenaWell , our care management app, patients can easily stay on track with their care plan and enter data to keep you informed..
Here’s how we do it.
Data management.
Thanks to our deep experience aggregating complex data sources, you can be confident you have the right data at the right time to make better population health decisions. We advise you on which data sources are most relevant and useful based on your business goals. Then we organize your data so it’s easy to use, regardless of source. The result: a 360-degree view of network utilization and of each patient that you can act on immediately..
See how New England Quality Care Alliance improved quality scores by  50% by studying aggregated population health data..
Read case study ›.
Quality management.
We offer intuitive quality management reporting and dashboards, informed by best practices from the athenahealth network, to maximize your quality performance. Our dashboards help you uncover patient-level care gaps to optimize pre-visit planning workflows, and target patient outreach to bring patients in for needed care. Our Population Health services include customized, automated outreach campaigns to close care gaps..
On average, our clients have outperformed participating ACO organizations on 25 of 33 MSSP quality measures and both composite measures..
See what made 1,039 Capital Health patients schedule a wellness visit within 2 weeks of an outreach campaign..
Read case study ›.
Care management.
Better care for your patient population. More efficient utilization of your care management team. We help you reach more patients, more effectively..
Our curated library of evidence-based care management content includes comprehensive patient care plans with assessments, goals, and opportunities to engage patients in their own health. Our care management platform gives care managers a consolidated view of patients, including quality programs and utilization patterns, and provides transparency across sites of care to facilitate better, more efficient coordination..
The athenaWell.
app gives you visibility into patient activities between visits and empowers patients to report on their own progress. This added insight and efficiency helps you scale care management across more risk programs and address rising risk populations..
See how Heywood Healthcare reduced behavioral health repeat ED visits by 14% with improved visibility and care management..
Read case study ›.
Patient management.
With millions of lives covered on athenahealth Population Health, we know what keeps patients engaged—and so will you. Our fully integrated services allow patients, family members, and care teams to stay connected..
Automated patient outreach capabilities help you reach patients at scale, without added work for your staff. Outcomes are tracked at each stage, using best practices from across our network to maximize patient engagement and improve clinical outcomes. The athenaWell app keeps patients engaged with their care team and active in their own care. Patients can access—and self-report on—their care plan at their convenience, increasing patient engagement and care plan adherence..
See how Privia Medical Group reached 960 unique patients with an automated campaign, closing 3,000 or more care gaps and saving 128 hours of manual outreach work..
