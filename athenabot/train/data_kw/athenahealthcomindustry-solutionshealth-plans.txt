Improved operations We automate, facilitate, and minimize your most burdensome operational work, like claims submissions, remittance, and eligibility checking. The result? Reduced costs. Less friction between health plans and providers. Happier members. Seamless clinical data exchange We facilitate easier collaboration between health plans and providers by sharing clinical data in an automated, standardized electronic format. Your health plan will have the insights you need to provide high quality care at a lower cost—without manual chart audits. And because we can share data bi-directionally, you can close care gaps and drive quality by providing actionable insights in the moments of care. Quality program performance We embed your quality and risk programs in our providers’ workflow, helping you improve quality program ratings, risk adjustment and stratification, and program adoption.
